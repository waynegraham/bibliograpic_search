  • 43 (1773) 182-184 L "Critical Observations on some Similies in Pope."

    Richard Gough [?] or Rev. John Kynaston [?].

    Orig. "Q."; Sherbo 97-a: 127 attributes this item to Kynaston, who (dating from Wigan, Lancs., or from Caerhaes, Cornwall) frequently used the signature "Q."; but the contribution could equally well be by Richard Gough, who also used the signature "Q." many times during the same time period. In the absence of place-name dating or definitive internal evidence, this item is thus assigned tentatively to either Richard Gough or Rev. John Kynaston.
